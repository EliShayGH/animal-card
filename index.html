Hyacinth Macaw

With a length of 100cm head to tail the Hyacinth Macaw is the largest of the parrots in the world

  • Scientific Name: Anodorhynchus hyacinthinus
  • Average Length: 100 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Habitat: Central and Eastern South America

Striking cobalt blue feathers, contrasting with the bare yellow eye ring and yellow patch of skin next to the lower bill. The tail is particularly long, and the powerful black bill is deeply curved and pointed
